Worked Examples

Two fully solved problems showing every divided difference calculation, polynomial construction, and final evaluation — exactly as you would on exam paper.

① Quadratic Interpolation

n = 3 points degree 2 polynomial
Problem: Given the data points \((1,\,3),\; (2,\,11),\; (4,\,47)\), construct the Newton interpolating polynomial and find \(P(3)\).
1
Identify the data
\[x_0=1,\; y_0=3 \qquad x_1=2,\; y_1=11 \qquad x_2=4,\; y_2=47\]
2
First-order divided differences
\[f[x_0,x_1]=\frac{f(x_1)-f(x_0)}{x_1-x_0}=\frac{11-3}{2-1}=\frac{8}{1}=8\] \[f[x_1,x_2]=\frac{f(x_2)-f(x_1)}{x_2-x_1}=\frac{47-11}{4-2}=\frac{36}{2}=18\]
3
Second-order divided difference
\[f[x_0,x_1,x_2]=\frac{f[x_1,x_2]-f[x_0,x_1]}{x_2-x_0}=\frac{18-8}{4-1}=\frac{10}{3}\approx3.\overline{3}\]
4

Highlighted cells are the polynomial coefficients \(a_0, a_1, a_2\).

5
Construct the Newton polynomial
\[P_2(x)=a_0+a_1(x-x_0)+a_2(x-x_0)(x-x_1)\] \[P_2(x)=3+8(x-1)+\tfrac{10}{3}(x-1)(x-2)\]
6
Evaluate at x = 3
\[P_2(3)=3+8(3-1)+\tfrac{10}{3}(3-1)(3-2)\] \[=3+8(2)+\tfrac{10}{3}(2)(1)=3+16+\tfrac{20}{3}=19+6.6\overline{6}\]
\[\boxed{P_2(3) \approx 25.6667}\]

② Cubic Interpolation

n = 4 points degree 3 polynomial
Problem: Given the data points \((0,\,2),\; (1,\,3),\; (2,\,12),\; (5,\,147)\), determine the Newton polynomial of degree 3 and evaluate it at \(x=3\).
1
Identify the data
\[x_0=0,\;y_0=2 \quad x_1=1,\;y_1=3 \quad x_2=2,\;y_2=12 \quad x_3=5,\;y_3=147\]
2
First-order divided differences
\[f[x_0,x_1]=\frac{3-2}{1-0}=1 \qquad f[x_1,x_2]=\frac{12-3}{2-1}=9 \qquad f[x_2,x_3]=\frac{147-12}{5-2}=\frac{135}{3}=45\]
3
Second-order divided differences
\[f[x_0,x_1,x_2]=\frac{9-1}{2-0}=\frac{8}{2}=4 \qquad f[x_1,x_2,x_3]=\frac{45-9}{5-1}=\frac{36}{4}=9\]
4
Third-order divided difference
\[f[x_0,x_1,x_2,x_3]=\frac{9-4}{5-0}=\frac{5}{5}=1\]
5

Construct the Newton polynomial
\[P_3(x)=2+1\cdot(x-0)+4\cdot(x-0)(x-1)+1\cdot(x-0)(x-1)(x-2)\] \[P_3(x)=2+x+4x(x-1)+x(x-1)(x-2)\]
7
Evaluate at x = 3
\[P_3(3)=2+(3)+4(3)(3-1)+(3)(3-1)(3-2)\] \[=2+3+4(3)(2)+(3)(2)(1)=5+24+6=35\]
\[\boxed{P_3(3)=35}\]
